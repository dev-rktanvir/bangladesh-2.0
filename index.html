Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Driving creativity and modern solutions to shape a forward-looking Bangladesh.

Sustainability

Building an eco-friendly and resilient nation for future generations.

Infrastructure

Creating smart cities, modern transport, and digital connectivity across Bangladesh.

Empowerment

Empowering citizens through education, technology, and equal opportunities.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Leveraging technology to create a connected, efficient, and innovative Bangladesh for all citizens.

Green Energy

Promoting renewable energy solutions to power a sustainable and eco-friendly future for the nation.

Modern Infrastructure

Developing smart cities, efficient transport, and world-class facilities to support rapid growth.

"Bangladesh stands at the threshold of a new era—an era where innovation, compassion, and collective effort will drive our nation forward. Bangladesh 2.0 is not just a vision, but a commitment to create opportunities for every citizen, harness the power of technology, protect our environment, and build a future where equality and dignity remain at the center of development."

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
